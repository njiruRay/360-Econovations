Our Story

Pioneering the circular economy in Embu, Kenya, by transforming tomorrow's waste into today's value.

Turning waste into value

At 360 Econovations Enterprise, we believe that waste is simply a resource in the wrong place. Our journey began in Embu, driven by a vision to solve local environmental challenges through industrial efficiency and communal empowerment.

We operate at the intersection of logistical complexity and environmental stewardship. By creating robust systems for collection and processing, we're not just cleaning neighborhoods; we're establishing a sustainable engine for local green jobs and economic resilience.

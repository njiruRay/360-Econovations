A Cleaner Way to Cook

Our biomass briquettes are engineered for peak performance. By utilizing sawdust, agricultural husks, and charcoal fines, we create a high-density fuel source that burns longer and hotter than traditional firewood or charcoal. This circular economy approach prevents waste from clogging landfills and reduces pressure on Kenya's local forests.

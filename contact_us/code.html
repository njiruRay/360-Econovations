Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you deliver?

expand_more

We currently offer delivery services across Embu County and select neighboring regions. For bulk industrial recycling equipment, we offer nationwide shipping within Kenya.

Is payment on delivery available?

expand_more

Yes, we support Cash on Delivery for orders within Embu town. For outside regions, we accept M-Pesa payments and Bank transfers before dispatching items.

How can I place an order?

expand_more

You can place an order directly through our online Shop, via our WhatsApp support line, or by visiting our facility in Embu. Our team is ready to guide you through the process.
